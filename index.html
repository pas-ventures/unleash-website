Who's Behind This

Meet Phil

Builder. Investor. Long South Africa.

Philipp Ströhemann first landed in Cape Town in 2014 for a business school exchange — and never really left. He co-founded his first company with a South African partner that same year.

Over 15 years, Phil has built and scaled multiple businesses across B2B SaaS, marketplaces, and tech-enabled services — from founding to exit. He's been through the full cycle: bootstrapping, building sales teams, scaling revenue, and selling to private equity.

Now he's putting that experience — and his own capital — behind the next generation of South African founders. Not as a fund manager, but as someone who shows up, rolls up his sleeves, and works alongside you on GTM, sales, pricing, hiring, and execution.

The ecosystem doesn't need more advice. It needs people who have done it.

What We Believe

Our Principles

This is not another venture fund. This is a community of builders who invest their own money, share what they've learned, and show up when it matters.

Community, Not a Fund

No external LPs. No fund structure. We invest our own capital alongside founders. Full alignment, full skin in the game.

Experience, Not Advice

Every insight comes from having built, scaled, and exited. Not from reading about it. From doing it.

Radical Alignment

Same shares. No preferred stock. No liquidation preferences. We only win when founders win.

Funded for SA Reality

SA tech startups need to be funded for South African reality and for flexibility — not shoehorned into Silicon Valley playbooks.

Cash-Flow First

SA rewards efficiency, not burn. Companies reach customers and revenue early — without fundraising dependency.

Founder Ownership

Realistic valuations. Founders keep real ownership and achieve real economic outcomes. No zombie companies.

How It Works

From Application to Access

One application. One quarter to prove engagement. Then you're in.
1

Apply

Fill in the application properly. Tell us about your business, your stage, and what you're working on. Be thorough — it matters.

2

We Review

Our team reviews every application personally. We're looking for founders building real businesses, not pitch decks.

3

Community Access

Accepted founders join a community built around mentorship, peer groups, and experts with a track record. Access to all events, our network, and our mentors.

4

One Quarter to Show Engagement

You get a full quarter. Show up, contribute, execute. Your engagement pre-qualifies you for continued access in the next quarter.

5

Ongoing Access & Deeper Engagement

Founders who show up earn continued access. Deeper investment or advisory engagement comes from the relationship — not the application.
