Community Guidelines

The Unleash Community

A free community for South African early-stage founders. Quarterly cohorts, honest feedback, operator mentorship. This page sets out how it works and what we ask of you.

01 — What it is

What Unleash is

A free community for South African early-stage founders.

You get access to Phil and our mentors, a WhatsApp peer group — 15 founders per cohort — our playbooks and tools, and three community calls each quarter. Calls are led by the founders in the room, working through real problems in real time.

Pre-revenue hunting product-market fit, scaling sales, working toward a co-founder hire, or preparing your next round — the cohort works on it with you.

You are part of your cohort.

02 — What it isn't

What Unleash is not

  • Not a VC, not a fund. We provide value whether we invest or not.
  • Not an accelerator. No curriculum, no demo day, no equity.
  • Not a pitch room. The point is the work, not introductions. Show up to move forward, not to pitch.
03 — The why

Why we do this

Building a company costs less than it used to. Leaner teams. Faster software. Less capital needed before you have real signal.

That changes the order of operations.

Isolate a real problem. Get in front of customers early. Get to revenue before you raise. Build cash-resilient from day one.

Mentorship from people who've built and sold — not consultants, not coaches.

South African funds look for revenue. So does every serious investor. Build to that standard.

04 — Who's behind it

Who's behind this

Unleash is run by PAS Ventures — Philipp Ströhemann's family holding, which finances the initiative — together with the supporters and mentors we've met along the way.

Two things we're trying to do.

Get as many South African companies as possible to self-sustaining revenue — or to funding that actually fits: revenue-based, angel, operator money. Not every business needs VC.

See the best businesses early. Some we'll back through our studio model. Most we won't. A founder who spends a quarter here should be further along — whether we invest or not.

05 — How it works

Quarterly cohorts

  • 15 founders per cohort.
  • Each cohort runs for one quarter.
  • Three community calls per quarter. Attend at least two.
  • You can stay in the community for multiple quarters — as long as you hit two of three calls each quarter. If you don't, your spot goes to another founder. We want to give value to as many people as possible.
  • Applications for the next cohort open the last month of the current quarter.
06 — WhatsApp norms

How we use the WhatsApp group

  • Tell us what you're working on, what's been hard, and what you've been solving.
  • Don't wait for the community call — Phil, the mentors, and other founders will try to unblock you right there.
  • Share progress when it's useful to others.
  • Speak from experience. Keep it straight.
07 — The deal

What we commit to you

By the end of your first quarter in the community:

  1. A clear view of your entrepreneurial journey and your business.
  2. An honest assessment from Phil and our co-mentors.
  3. A clear picture of your funding options.
  4. If it's an investment opportunity for Unleash, we'll say so — and if not, why.
08 — In return

What we ask from you

  • Engage fully. Make this useful for you.
  • Use the tools we share — playbooks, templates, skills.
  • Tell us what we can improve. Email Nicolene at nicolene@pas-ventures.com with what you'd like to see.
  • Help other founders. Be open.
  • If you know a founder this is right for, send them our application link: tally.so/r/7Rx9z2
