Community Guidelines

Welcome to the Unleash Community

Your support cohort for the quarter. A free community for early-stage South African founders, run by Unleash Ventures. Our goal, what you get, and how the three months work.

Phil walks you through how the Unleash Community works · ~5 min

01 — Why we're here

Why we're here

Our goal is to get South African entrepreneurship unleashed. This community is how we do it.

Unleashed means actionable feedback for founders on the ground, from people who've actually built and sold companies, delivered through an online community you can reach from anywhere.

We'll be straight about the second reason too: we're growing our investment portfolio, and this is where we meet the founders we want to back.

02 — What you get

What you get

Everything here is free for early-stage founders. You get:

  • A WhatsApp group of founders at the same stage as you.
  • Two open Q&A calls a month, shaped around the real problems coming up in the group.
  • One-on-one sparring with Phil for the ventures that fit the Unleash framework — founders building camels, not unicorns. You apply for this.
  • A members section with everything we learn on the Q&A calls, plus the AI tools we've built for this.
  • Templates for the hard parts — validating your idea, going to market, building your pitch deck.
03 — Who's in the room

Who's in the room

These are your peers. The community is for early-stage founders, which usually means:

  • You're early — pre-revenue, or with a handful of design partners, and mostly under R2M in revenue on your new venture.
  • You're open to feedback and want to learn from other founders.
  • You're working out the best way forward — including what kind of investment, if any, to take.
04 — How it works

How it works

You can stay in the WhatsApp group as long as you like, and you decide how many Q&A calls you join.

To give each venture proper attention and structured feedback, we run the community in quarterly cohorts. The next one starts 1 July.

05 — What happens next

What happens next

  1. You apply to the Unleash Community.
  2. The community team reviews it and messages you when you're approved.
  3. We add you to the WhatsApp group.
  4. We ask you to sign up to our platform and introduce yourself.
  5. You're invited to every Q&A — come to the next one.
  6. If you want feedback on your venture, fill out the Venture Assessment.
06 — The program

Your three months: assess, execute, decide

Month 1 — Assess

  • Join the WhatsApp group.
  • Fill out the Unleash assessment (you'll find it in the members section) and send it to us.
  • We'll get you feedback as fast as we can, and tell you if Phil or the team wants to meet you one-on-one.
  • If you're stuck before then, bring it to the group or join a Q&A call.

Month 2 — Execute

  • By now you've had feedback, and two or three things worth focusing on.
  • We help you work through them — on the Q&A calls and by answering your questions in the group. Don't hold back, and keep tracking your progress.

Month 3 — Decide

  • We look at where you've got to, and which blockers are still there — some always are, and that's normal.
  • Then we decide together: keep working with you one-on-one (which means we're looking at investing), or point you to the people who can take you further.

The whole way through, we track progress together on our progress sheet (Nicolene will reach out about it), so we can see how everyone's doing and help you keep moving.

07 — What we ask

What we ask of you

  • Fill out the assessment if you want feedback on your venture.
  • Show up for the Q&A calls and bring your questions. Come prepared.
  • Get stuck in.
  • Keep your weekly progress updated. We'll remind you in the WhatsApp group — it shows us what to prioritise for the next Q&A.
08 — How we show up

How we show up

  • Keep it confidential. We want people to share openly, and we expect everyone to honour that.
  • No pitching, no spam. We do want to hear about your venture — just keep side offers out of it and keep it professional.
  • Help where you can, and share your progress.
  • Stay active in the WhatsApp group — it's where all our communication happens. Bring the questions you're stuck on.
09 — Staying in

Staying in the community

We want to keep in touch with founders for the long term and grow this community. The community stays open to you as long as you like. The only things we ask:

  • If you want dedicated attention from the Unleash team, share your assessment with us.
  • If you just want to stay on for the next quarter, fill out the quarterly survey. Tell us how you did and where you made progress — it keeps us sharp on what to build next.

Welcome to the Unleash Community

We'll give you a straight read throughout — on the business, on what's missing, on whether Unleash is the right next step for you. We'll only say it if we'd back it ourselves. Glad to have you in the room.
