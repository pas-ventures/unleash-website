Community Guidelines

Welcome to the Unleash Community

A free community for early-stage founders, run by Unleash Ventures — the investment studio of Philipp Alexander Ströhemann. This is how the community works, and what we ask of you.

01 — What you get

What you get

It's free to join. This is what's included:

  • A WhatsApp group of founders at the same stage as you.
  • Open Q&A calls every two weeks — bring a real problem and work through it with the group.
  • One-on-one sparring with Phil, plus a network of operators who've built and sold real companies.
  • A members section: our playbooks, tools, and the AI Venture Assessment.
  • A peer cohort that runs the three-month program together.
02 — Why we do this

Why we do this

South Africa has founders willing to build. What it mostly lacks is support from people who've actually done it — built something, sold it, kept going. That's the gap we're trying to close.

There's a second reason, and we'll be straight about it: we're growing our investment portfolio, and this is where we find the founders we want to back.

03 — Who it's for

Who it's for

A fit usually looks like one or more of these:

  • Pre-revenue, up to R2 million in annual revenue.
  • You're open to feedback.
  • You want to build lean and get the right feedback before you raise.
  • You're looking for a mentor, or for funding.
  • You've found a real problem with a real customer, and you want to build it out.
04 — The program

Three months: assess, execute, decide

Month 1 — Assess

  • You join the WhatsApp group.
  • Run the Venture Assessment if you want feedback on your business.
  • Together we find the two or three blockers that, once cleared, will unleash the business.
  • You start posting your weekly progress on the dashboard — and keep that up through Month 2.

Month 2 — Execute

  • A month to work on those blockers, with nothing new added on top.

Month 3 — Decide and next steps

  • We work out the right path together.
  • If you're a fit for investment through Unleash, we'll tell you.
  • If you're a better fit for another investor, we'll tell you that too — and what's still missing for you to raise.
05 — Keeping it alive

How we keep it alive

  • Post your weekly progress on the dashboard. The founders who show up consistently get the most from the community.
  • Use the founder directory — who everyone is, and what they can help you with.
  • Point people — a few founders help run the odd Q&A or share what's working. If that's you, tell us.
06 — How we show up

How we show up

  • Keep it confidential. What founders share here stays inside the community.
  • No pitching, no spam.
  • Be open and helpful.
  • Show up as yourself, and be honest about what you're stuck on — that's the only way we can actually help you.
07 — What happens next

What happens next

  1. You apply to the Unleash Community.
  2. The community team reviews it and messages you when you're approved.
  3. We add you to the WhatsApp group.
  4. We ask you to sign up to our platform and introduce yourself.
  5. You're invited to every Q&A — come to the next one.
  6. If you want feedback on your venture, fill out the Venture Assessment.
08 — The one ask

The one ask

The Unleash Community is free. In return, when your three months are up, we ask one thing:

  • Fill out the quarterly survey and keep your progress updated — that's what keeps your membership active and the community free.

Welcome to the Unleash Community

We'll give you a straight read throughout — on the business, on what's missing, on whether Unleash is the right next step for you. We'll only say it if we'd back it ourselves. Glad to have you in the room.
