Community Guidelines

The Unleash Community

The Unleash Community sits at the heart of Unleash Ventures — the investment studio of Philipp Alexander Ströhemann. A free, invite-only community for early-stage founders: the team brings you in when there's a match. This page sets out how it works and what we ask of you.

01 — What it is

What the Unleash Community is

A free community for early-stage founders. What that means in practice:

  • A WhatsApp group.
  • Open Q&A calls.
  • One-on-one sparring sessions with Phil.

Whether you're pre-revenue, hunting product-market fit, validating with your first customers, looking for funding, or getting a co-founder on board — the community works on it with you.

02 — What it isn't

What Unleash Ventures isn't

Unleash Ventures is the private investment firm of Philipp Ströhemann and his co-investors. The Unleash Community is a free part of it, not a funnel into it. So, to be clear:

  • Not a VC.
  • Not an accelerator. No demo days.
  • No equity taken to join.

The community is free, but it asks something of you: honest reflection, introspection, and collaboration. Only founders who stay active stay in.

03 — Who it's for

Who the Unleash Community is for

Good early-stage founders. A fit usually looks like one or more of these:

  • You're early — pre-revenue, or with your first revenue coming in.
  • You want to build a camel, not a unicorn — cash-efficient, founder-controlled.
  • You'd rather prove you can charge money first.
  • You've found a real problem and want to build it out.

We're invite-only — the team brings founders in when there's a match.

04 — What you get

What you get

  • The investment studio — Phil, Nicolene and the Unleash team, reading your venture and giving you an honest read.
  • Phil and our network of operators who've built and sold real companies.
  • The WhatsApp community and open Q&A calls every two weeks.
  • The peer cohort.
  • The members section.
  • The AI Venture Assessment.
05 — The program

Three months: assess, execute, decide

Month 1 — Assess

  • You join the WhatsApp group.
  • Run the Venture Assessment if you want feedback on your business.
  • Together we find the two or three blockers that, once cleared, will truly unleash your business.
  • You start posting your weekly progress on the dashboard.

Month 2 — Execute

  • A month to build — no new busywork. You work on those two or three blockers.
  • You keep posting your weekly progress on the dashboard.

Month 3 — Decide and next steps

  • We work out the right path together.
  • If you're a fit for investment through Unleash, we'll tell you.
  • If you're a better fit for another investor, we'll tell you that too — and what's still missing for you to raise.
06 — Keeping it alive

How we keep the community alive

  • Post your weekly progress. The most active founders get the most attention from us, so show up on the dashboard.
  • Use the founder directory — who everyone is, and what they can help you with.
  • Point people — a few founders help run the odd Q&A or share their experience. If that's you, tell us.
07 — How we show up

How we show up

  • Keep it confidential. What's shared here stays here.
  • No pitching, no spam.
  • Be open and helpful.
  • Show up as yourself, and be open about your challenges. That's how we can actually help.
08 — The one ask

The one ask

The Unleash Community is free. When your three months are up, we ask one thing:

  • Fill out the quarterly survey and keep your progress updated.

That's how you stay in — and it's why the community can stay free.

09 — Our promise

Our promise to you

We'll always give you our honest read — even when it's not what you want to hear. We put our own capital into founders we believe in, so when we tell you something's working or it isn't, it's because we'd back it ourselves.

There's a lot of founder talent in South Africa that never finds the right room early enough. We're trying to change that.

Welcome to the Unleash Community

Free to join, commitment required. Glad to have you in the room. Know a founder who belongs here? Send them our way.
