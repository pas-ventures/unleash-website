Privacy & data

How your conversation is handled

While you're chatting:
Your messages are sent to Anthropic (Claude) so Luna can respond. Anthropic processes them under its standard API terms and does not train on them. We do not log or store your conversation on Unleash servers.

When you click "I'm done":
Luna assembles the structured Venture Assessment. We email it via Resend to you, Phil (pas@pas-ventures.com), and Nicolene (nicolene@pas-ventures.com). That's where your data lives from then on — in those three inboxes.

Local browser save:
Your in-progress conversation is saved to your own browser (localStorage) for up to 14 days so you can resume. It never leaves your device until you send. You can wipe it any time by clicking "start over".

GDPR & your rights:
Unleash Ventures is operated by PAS Ventures GmbH (Berlin, Germany). To request access, correction, or deletion of your Venture Assessment, email pas@pas-ventures.com.

This is a beta tool. We're refining the privacy posture continuously.
