    The bootstrap-viability thesis

    Without VC fuel, every new customer either funds the next one within 1-3 months — or extends your time-to-zero. There is no middle ground on a long enough runway.

    Green · ACV > €10K / R100K+
    Bootstrap workable
    CAC payback hits 1-3 months if discipline holds. Each customer funds the next. Cash compounds.
    Yellow · ACV €5-10K / R50-100K
    Borderline
    Survivable only with brutal CAC discipline. Most founders here under-report CAC by 3-5×. Real numbers usually push this into red.
    Red · ACV < €5K / R50K
    Bootstrap math breaks
    VC-backed peers can absorb 12-18 month paybacks. You can't. Either raise ACV, slash CAC to near zero (PLG / referral), or raise capital.
