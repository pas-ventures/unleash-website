The Problem

Asset Foundations are the foundation of everything — but nobody does them properly.

01
THE OLD WAY
Listen to customer calls manually
Super daunting. You need to be a great listener, a great note-taker, and a great synthesizer. Most people aren’t all three.
02
THE BOTTLENECK
Founders skip it entirely
It’s so much work that most founders write landing pages, ads and scripts from gut feel instead. The foundation is missing — everything downstream suffers.
03
THE NEW WAY
Feed dozens or hundreds of calls at once
Way more data, way faster. The Asset Foundations you get are so good that everything you build on top of them just works.
